Training
Training will be considered as a pre-conference event (9-10 May).

Training cost
Regular: NRs 10,000.00 (NRs. Ten Thousands only)

Discount: Students can apply for the student discount as well. There will be NRs 2000 waive on the registration charge for the student (limited seats are available).

The cost includes
  • 2 Days PostgreSQL training session by an industry expert
  • Travel from Kathmandu pick up point to training venue
  • Light Breakfast, Lunch, Tea, Coffee and Water
  • Training Kit
  • Hands on notes and training materials
  • T-shirt with PostgreSQL logo etc.
Note: The bank account details for the payment will be shared separately on the email provided by participant.

Fill the registation form : Register for Training
Main Conference
The main event of the PostgreSQL Conference 2023 is the conference day (11-12 May).

Conference cost
For Nepalese Participants
- Early bird : NRs. 4000.00 First 15 registration
- Regular : NRs. 5000.00 Upto 30 participants
For Foreign Participants : USD 100

The cost includes
  • Pickup from Kathmandu pick up point
  • Tea/Coffee, Lunch and water at venue
  • Registration to the main conference and any parallel sessions
  • 1 Night stay at Dhulikhel where participants can network with each other and international delegates
  • Gala Dinner at Dhulikhel
  • Conference kit with T-Shirt and others
Note: The bank account details for the payment will be shared separately on the email provided by participant.

Fill the registation form : Register for Main Conference
