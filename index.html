Hello! PostgreSQL lovers

2 nd PostgreSQL Conference Nepal, 2023

Welcome to the second PostgreSQL Conference happening on 11-12 May 2023 in Nepal. After the successful organization of the first-ever PostgreSQL Conference Nepal in May 4-5, 2018, we are excited to announce this years conference. In the previous conference, we had quite a few exciting talks from internationally renowned and prominent personalities from the PostgreSQL community as well as from many local speakers.
We hope to continue the trend for this year and have similar support from the international community as well as locally.
